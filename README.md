# LAWGLIDER
## AI-Powered Multilingual Legal & Government Assistant
### *"Bridge Justice for Every Citizen"*

---

## 📌 Overview

LawGlider is an AI-powered multilingual legal accessibility platform using OLLAMA PHI3 MINI designed to bridge India's legal and governance communication gap. Millions of citizens struggle with legal documents, government schemes, and public services due to language barriers, legal jargon, lack of digital literacy, and unaffordable legal counsel.

LawGlider solves this problem by combining:

- AI-powered legal document simplification
- Multilingual regional language support
- Government scheme discovery
- 24/7 legal chatbot assistance

All within a simple, mobile-responsive platform.

---

# 🚨 Problem Statement

India's legal and governance ecosystem remains inaccessible to a large portion of the population.

### Key Challenges

- Legal documents are written in complex English and legal terminology.
- Citizens remain unaware of government welfare schemes.
- Government portals are difficult to navigate and mostly English-based.
- Legal consultation is expensive and inaccessible for rural populations.
- Senior citizens and first-time users struggle with digital systems.

These issues disproportionately affect:

- Rural citizens
- Women
- Senior citizens
- Daily wage workers
- Marginalized communities

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

LawGlider addresses these challenges through four major pillars:

## 1. Legal Document Simplification
Upload legal PDFs such as:

- FIRs
- Contracts
- Court notices

The AI instantly generates:

- Plain-language explanations
- Key clauses
- Rights & obligations
- Risks & next steps

---

## 2. Multilingual AI Legal Assistant

SimplyLaw AI responds in regional Indian languages including:

- Kannada
- Hindi
- Tamil
- Bengali
- English

This makes governance communication more accessible.

---



## 4. Government Scheme Finder

An intelligent recommendation system that helps citizens discover:

- Welfare schemes
- Subsidies
- Benefits
- Eligibility-based programs

Based on:

- State
- Category
- User profile
